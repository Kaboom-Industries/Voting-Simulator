Interpretation Guide

RRV Favored (Consensus)

Films that perform better in Range Voting usually have Broad Support (many 6s, 7s, 8s) but lack "Number One" votes. They are the "safe" choices that everyone agrees are good.

STV Favored (Passion)

Films that perform better in Preferential Voting have Deep Passion (high number of 10s/Rank #1s) but might be hated by others. STV rewards intensity over breadth.
